How I Built a Copy Trading System That Runs While I Sleep

I work 12-hour shifts driving trucks. When I get home, I'm exhausted. The last thing I want to do is stare at charts for 3 hours. But I also don't want to miss opportunities.

So I built a system that copies proven traders automatically. Here's exactly how I did it — and what it cost me.

Starting Point: $240

I had $240 in USDC sitting in a Hyperliquid wallet. Not enough to trade myself (I'd get rekt), but enough to copy someone who knows what they're doing.

The platform I chose was CoinPilot. It connects to Hyperliquid and lets you subscribe to lead traders. When they open a position, your account copies it automatically. When they close, you close. Zero manual intervention.

Finding the Right Lead Trader

This is the most important decision. A bad lead trader will lose your money faster than you can blink. Here's what I look for:

  • Win rate > 75% — Consistency beats home runs
  • Sortino > 1.5 — Risk-adjusted returns matter
  • Max drawdown < 5% — I can't stomach 50% drops
  • 200+ trades — Statistical significance
  • Active > 90 days — Not a flash in the pan

My first lead: 79.9% win rate, $46K account, 229 trades over 170 days. Total P&L: +$55,253. That's the kind of track record I want to copy.

The Setup

Platform: Hyperliquid via CoinPilot Lead 1: 0xdb7cc8d3... (79.9% win) Lead 2: 0xa1b6d8ef... (98.6% win) Mode: Fixed $20-50 per trade Stop Loss: 10% Check frequency: Once per day

I set it to FIXED mode (not proportional). This means every trade is $20-50 regardless of account size. Safer than proportional, which can blow up if the lead uses huge leverage.

Monitoring (The Fun Part)

I built a custom monitoring system that checks my copy trading status every day. It reports:

  • Current balance & P&L
  • Lead trader stats (win rate, drawdown)
  • Open positions
  • SOL/DOWGE price alerts

All delivered to my Telegram. I check it during my lunch break. Takes 30 seconds.

Lessons Learned

Lesson 1: Don't panic at red. My lead got liquidated once. I lost $3.02. But the system is designed to survive drawdowns. Patience wins.

Lesson 2: Diversify leads. One lead with 98.6% win rate and another with 79.9% gives me exposure to different strategies.

Lesson 3: Fixed position sizing is safer. Proportional mode would have me risking $100+ per trade by now. Fixed keeps me disciplined.
