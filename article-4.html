From Truck Driver to AI Builder: My Story

I'm 27 years old. I drive trucks for a living — 12-hour shifts, home every day, weekends off. I make okay money. But I want more. Not just money. Freedom.

This is how I went from knowing nothing about code to building AI systems that run while I sleep.

The Beginning (Age 15)

I started working at 15. Saved $10,000 by age 21. Not because I was smart — because I was consistent. Worked hard. Spent little. That $10K became the seed money for everything that followed.

At 19, something clicked. I stopped smoking weed. Started working out. Started eating better. Started caring about my future.

The College Years

College is where I made my first real friends. Started businesses with them. Learned that I could figure things out if I just started. Didn't wait for permission. Didn't wait to feel ready.

First business funded with that $10K savings. Failed. Learned. Tried again.

The Truck Job

Now I drive trucks. Local routes, home every night. It's not glamorous. But it pays the bills. And it gives me something most people don't have: predictable time.

I know exactly when I work and when I'm free. That predictability is leverage. I use my evenings and weekends to build.

Discovering AI (2025)

I heard about ChatGPT like everyone else. Tried it. Was amazed. Started paying $20/month. Then $40/month. Then $60/month as I added more tools.

But something bothered me: I was renting my tools. If I stopped paying, I lost everything. My workflows, my data, my automations — all tied to someone else's platform.

The Pivot to Local

I discovered Ollama. Local AI models. Free. Private. Owned by me.

The learning curve was steep. I spent weeks figuring out installations, configurations, model downloads. Failed more times than I can count. But each failure taught me something.

"I don't have talent. I have persistence. I show up every day and do the work. That's the only advantage I need."

Building Cortex (2026)

Cortex started as a personal assistant. Now it's a multi-agent system with 5 specialized AI agents:

  • Coder — writes and debugs code
  • Researcher — finds alpha and opportunities
  • Writer — generates content and copy
  • Trader — monitors markets and alerts
  • Organizer — manages files and receipts

All running 24/7. All costing $0. All on my machine.

The Current State

Day job: Truck driver (12hr shifts)

Evenings: Building AI systems, copy trading, content creation

Systems built: Receipt tracker, copy trading monitor, content pipeline, multi-agent system

Monthly AI costs: $0

Debt: Still $45K. But now I have tools that can help me earn my way out.

Why I Share This

I'm not special. I don't have a computer science degree. I didn't go to Stanford. I'm a truck driver who decided to learn.

If I can build this, you can too. Or I can build it for you. Either way, the tools exist. The knowledge is free. The only question is whether you'll start.

"The best time to start was yesterday. The second best time is now."

What's Next

My goal is simple: build systems that generate income while I sleep. Then scale them. Then teach others to do the same.

The receipt tracker is my first product. The AI setup service is my second. More coming.

I'm not building an empire overnight. I'm building it one evening at a time.
